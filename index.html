Frequently asked questions

Can't find the answer youre looking for? Send us an email and we'll get back to you as soon as we can

What does a typical project with Atlas Technology look like?
We start with an introductory call in which we decide whether we want to work together. After that we scope out the project and begin the design and implementation phase. During the whole project we frequently report on our progress and collect feedback, following our iterative, incremental approach to software development.
What projects have you worked on in the past?
The projects our engineers have worked include financial risk modelling, simulation of event flows, blockchain apps and smart contracts, and business intelligence (BI) tools. We have delivered software and IP in exploratory R&D settings as well as for critical user-facing systems and we can employ a wide range of testing and verification methods.
What is your policy on open-source software?
We support open-source software where we can, for example by contributing changes to upstream repositories or by publishing documentation.
When can you get started?
We aim to provide the resources you need within three weeks of agreeing to work together. However, we understand that some problems need to be solved right away, and we can offer shorter timeframes if required.
Do you work remotely or on-site?
Our implementation work is done remotely, but we frequently meet our clients on-site because we believe there is no better way to communicate about technology than with everyone in the same room.
